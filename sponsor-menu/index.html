Brewing Agile - Sponsor Menu

This year we will be accepting sponsorship in two package types. The basics of each package are listed below, however if you have any special requirements or questions please don’t hesitate to contact us at brewingagile@scrumbeers.com.

Bottle Sponsor - 6 000 SEK

The basic sponsorship package includes:

  • Logo and link on our website. www.brewingagile.org.
  • Logo on all Brewing Agile powerpoint slides shows during the event.
  • Logo included in all promotional material distributed.
  • One seat at the conference. Does not include workshop addon.
  • Thank you from the organizers during the conference for your sponsorship.

On Tap Sponsor - 12 000 SEK

The full sponsorship package includes:

  • Logo and link on our website. www.brewingagile.org
  • Logo on all Brewing Agile powerpoint slides shows during the event.
  • Logo included in all promotional material distributed.
  • Rollup and stand at the event for networking and software demo’s.
  • Two seats at the conference. Does not include workshop addon.
  • Up to 5 minutes to speak about your organization from the main stage.
  • Option to distribute handouts to all event participants.
    • A great way to advertise or recruit for your organization!
    • Handout must be provided by sponsor.

All prices are excluding VAT.
